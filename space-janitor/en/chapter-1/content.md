Nothing but emptiness and blackness yawns at you through the cockpit window of the Janitor. At breakneck speed, the wooden spaceship plows through the galaxy. The massive, space-resistant planks creak familiarly under your feet.
Suddenly, a pungent smell of cheap bourbon stings your nose, followed by a wet smack next to you. You shoot a hard glance to the side. Hank wipes his grinning mouth across his shaggy, gray beard and shakes his head. "Relax, buddy," your co-pilot slurrs. "We won't arrive for a few hours anyway. A good sip bridges the time."
You forcefully reach for the bottle. Hank growls like a wild dog and sinks his teeth painfully into your forearm. You gasp, grab his beard and yank at it, prompting him to put you in a headlock with a loud yelp. The sour smell of your buddy's sweat burns your nose as you both stumble and crash hard against the cockpit console.
Your hand accidentally disengages the autopilot, while Hank's elbow jerks the flight stick around. The violent shaking makes you taste blood on your lip. A deafening crack tears through the creaking wood – an asteroid impact. Air escapes with a shrill whistle, and the cockpit is bathed in the flickering light of red alarm bells.
"Hull breach!" Hank screams, clutching his seat pale as a ghost while the vacuum suction tears at your clothes. "What do we do now?!"
